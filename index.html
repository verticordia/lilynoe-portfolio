1. Collins Heights
Applied Architectural Technology
2022


The Collins Heights is a 250m tall 51-storey tower taking over the southern portion of the site. It is a mixed-use skyscraper that consists of 32 office levels, 15 hotel floors, 4 open-air mechanical floors, a sky lobby, and a crown. The form aims for a tall, narrow tower that goes up parallel to the Rialto tower with 251m.

lily.thinmyatnoe@gmail.com
Linkedin / InstagramAs a graduate of architecture, I have developed a strong design sensibility and a deep understanding of architectural principles. Also driven by curiosity, I expanded my skillset beyond the standard architecture curriculum by exploring Archviz, VR game design, drone photogrammetry, BIM, and GIS.  These approaches are evident in my works.

Throughout my academic journey, I have cultivated a keen design eye and a deep understanding of architectural principles. My passions in architecture lie in:
  • humanistic qualities and phenomenology in architecture.
  • creating ecocentric designs that engage both human and non-human needs with ESD principles.
  • design that focuses on non-conforming bodies to promote new forms of interdependent care
  • sustainable rehabilitation and adaptive reuse of abandoned or underutilized heritage buildings.

This combination of expertise and curiosity defines my confidence in my ability to create meaningful, sustainable, and humane spaces.
